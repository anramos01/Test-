Nomad Rentals

Ramos, Angel

Purpose and Goals

Nomad Rentals’ goal is to provide a platform for showing how to passively manage rental properties. The website will feature rental properties, business information, and contact options. The URL to my webiste is: nomadrentals.com.

Target Audience

The target audience includes real estate investors and individuals interested in passive income through rental properties. They will expect detailed guides, property management tips, and investment strategies. The site will offer an informative and user-friendly experience to help investors manage their rental properties without having to do too much.

Competitor Analysis with Examples

reination:
  • Passive real estate investing involves owning properties without having to manage them actively. It enables investors to generate income from real estate while avoiding the responsibilities of being a landlord, (Hayes, 2024).
  • REI Nation is a reliable source and a huge competitor because it offers many examples and scenarios. They are a true passive real estate investing company. They buy distressed homes in average and above neighborhoods, renovate and sell to investors that are looking for passive income and to diversify their portfolio. It's a complete package, they can help with finding the mortgage lenders, lawyers, accounting, CPA'a and they are also the property management.
BiggerPockets:
  • Bigger Pockets is a board-based real estate investment website. They are growing bigger every year due to the amount of support they provide. They also have a podcast BiggerPockets which is highly recommended for beginners to very experienced. The podcast brings in guests that share their experience of the good, the bad, and the ugly. Going back to the website also provides a thread for questions, people’s comments/stories about their investments. All provide educational resources such as books, forms, legal, formulas, and anything that can help you learn. The website layout is easy to use and browse around.

Content Strategy

The website will feature articles, images of properties, formulas, and examples. Content will be sourced from personal experience and industry experts (websites and blogs), focusing on passive property management strategies.

Font Families

For this website, I chose Montserrat for the headers because of its modern look which makes the headings stand out.

For the body copy, I selected Roboto due to its clean appearance, making the content easy to read.
